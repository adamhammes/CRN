Differential Equation to CRN Converter

Please enter each differential equation on a separate line with the affected species on the left side of the equation. Powers greater than 1 should be denoted with a carat (^), and each species name must be a single character, possibly followed by a nonnegative numeric index using an underscore (_). Non-numeric term coefficients must be a single character and must not clash with a defined species name.
